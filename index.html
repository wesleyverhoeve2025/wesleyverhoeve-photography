Wesley Verhoeve is an Amsterdam-based photographer, curator and writer.

His photography, rooted in human stories of craft and community, has been featured in The New York Times, National Geographic Traveler, Wired, and Het Parool.

As the founding curator of the Projected series at ICP in New York, he organized 96 exhibitions showcasing over 300 photographers from 53 countries.

He also publishes Process, a weekly newsletter on photography and creative work.
